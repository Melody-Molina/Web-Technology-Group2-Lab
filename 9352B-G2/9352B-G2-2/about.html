About Us

Substance Abuse Prevention and Rehabilitation, together with the Department of Health we work together to help the people in community to understand more about the the prevention abuse of substances.

Site Info

Substance Abuse Prevention and Rehabilitation is an open access, that is related to concerns about substance abuse with focus on a community together with its people.

Mission

To improve the physical, spiritual and emotional health of the alcoholic and chemically dependent person and his or her family through an integrated system of addiction medicine, nursing and counseling services based on a balanced program of patient care, education and performance measurement.

Vision

To provide an environment for the exchange of ideas, also critical reviews to established fields that share a mutual goal of reducing substance abuse.
