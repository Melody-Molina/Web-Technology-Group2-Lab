Substance Use Prevention

Substance use prevention programs and strategies should begin with the adolescents in a barangay.
Because adolescents at a young age, it can easily be used into substance use disorder.
Because of that mostly of the children in a barangay consider to be vulnerable to becoming dependent on those substances that will be adversely affect them.
So, the most important decisive timefor prevention programs should start with young children or adolescents before they have a notion in using of any substance.
In this case, the public institutions will be confident enough with their plans to implement it and will make sure that it will do good for the people.
The people will notice immediately or eventually the changes of the plans implemented and it will show if it has a good effect with the public affairs.


Teenagers and drugs and alcohol

The teenager years are the most one suspectible for experimentation and also taking their risks.
According to the Department of Health(DOH) teenager brains was still under construction.
It is also the critical time for the brain to change and also develop at the same time.
Their decisions should consider the concerns of the peoplea and also the teenagers will be able to do something about it.
They should make sure that what they are planning about is for the betterment of the community.
The barangay is obliged to let the people in community to know and always updated about what are being held like prevention programs and also forums.


Mental Health

Mental health is one of the basic fundamentals that affect number of people in a barangay.
Preventing behavioral and substances disorder in the community is critical to the mental health of people inside a community.
These disorder were one of the top reason for developing disabilities and were projected to surepass physical diseases as a major cause in a community.


Prevention of Substance Use

There are many ways or measures in order for a community toprevent substance use especially among the youth.
First of all, the parents should show their interest in their children's development and talk to them about any problems or concern they may have.
If the child is depressed, sad or having a thought of having a suicidal thoughts the parents must consult their children to a professional for a help.
Parents should also give the child love and also attention he/she needs and also spend a quality time with him/her children's.
The barangay must also encourage to participate with these concerns.


Showing interest and supporting the children/teenagers extracurricular activities is also important.
Parents and elders also must teach the children about on how to make good decision by themselves, and offer them an advice and if he needs a support.
Healthy, happy children's are the one that were less prone to drugs or alcohol, especially when they know the dangers of such actions by themselves.
The community will also be able to help each other if the local government will be an example to them.
