Women's Month Celebration

Last March 2017 Baguio celebrated Women's Month. It set a theme "We Make Change Work for Women". The theme calls for gender-balance when it comes to leadership and decision making positions. The said event highlighted women's achievements and discuss continuing and emerging women's empowerment and gender equality issues and concerns, challenges and commitments.

After the awarding ceremony. There was a parade followed by a march rally held in the Igorot Park.

Last March 10, the government conducted Teen Health Forum. which focuses to the health of the young adults which was held on Baguio Center for Young Adults and Save Our Women.

To close the celebrating theme, on March 31, there was an orientation to our Jeepney Operators and Drivers Association on the topic about the feature of the magna Carta for Women/Gender Based Violence and PWD Laws.
